Our Timeline

Brainstorming Ideas (October 13-15)

To start our project, we brainstormed many ideas as a team including studying the chemical properties of aspirin and its effect on the human body, studying the chemical basis of food additives, and an investigation of crystals and gemstones.

Choosing a Focus (October 16-21)

After brainstorming, we needed to narrow down our focus. We decided to investigate crystals and gemstones because this is a topic that all of us are very interested in, and it has many connections to the chemistry units we have studied in class including reactions, types of solids, and bonding.

Research (October 22-31)

After finalizing our topic, we started to research. We broke up the main topic into 4 subtopics â€“ natural crystals, synthetic crystals, diamonds, and other methods of creating crystals. Video Creation (November 1-10)

Once we finished our research, we recorded a video to present the information. Due to the current circumstances because of COVID-19, we each filmed parts of the video separately and edited it together to create a comprehensive piece to explain this topic.

Website Creation (November 1-10)

We also created a website to showcase our knowledge on this topic. The website contains separate pages for each of our subtopics, as well as the video we created, and our bibliography.
